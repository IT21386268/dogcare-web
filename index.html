About WOOF DOC

Revolutionizing canine healthcare through artificial intelligence

Project Overview

WOOF DOC is a cutting-edge, AI-powered web application designed to transform canine healthcare. Through the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), our platform offers a wide range of personalized services for dog health management.

Problem Addressed

Traditional veterinary care lacks real-time monitoring and proactive health management, often relying on occasional visits for checkups. This project leverages AI and ML to create a real-time, proactive healthcare system.

React Frontend Python FastAPI Supabase DB Machine Learning Computer Vision NLP

Key Features

Comprehensive AI-driven solutions for canine healthcare

Disease Detection & Medication

AI predicts nutrition-related diseases like obesity, tracks medication schedules, and sends reminders for timely treatment and health management.

  • Disease prediction and prevention.
  • Medication tracking with reminders.
  • Personalized health recommendations.

Advanced disease detection and medication tracking

Personalized Care Plans

AI creates tailored meal and exercise plans, adjusting as the dog's health evolves based on breed, age, and weight.

  • Custom meal and exercise plans.
  • Health-based adjustments in plans.
  • Breed, age, and weight-based recommendations.

Tailored nutrition and exercise plans

Virtual Veterinary Assistant

AI-powered assistant for 24/7 symptom analysis, immediate advice, and escalation to veterinarians when further consultation is needed.

  • 24/7 remote consultation.
  • Symptom analysis and advice.
  • Escalates to veterinarians when necessary.

24/7 AI-powered veterinary assistance

Skin Disease Detection

AI analyzes skin images to detect conditions like dermatitis, hotspots, and tumors, offering early diagnosis and treatment options.

  • Skin condition detection via AI.
  • Early disease identification.
  • Treatment suggestions based on analysis.

AI-powered skin condition analysis
